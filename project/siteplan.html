Website Planning Document

Site Name

MindForge AI — A website focused on introducing Artificial Intelligence to beginners and future developers.

Why this name?

This name represents the idea of shaping and building intelligence, just like a forge shapes metal. It connects perfectly with artificial intelligence, learning, and the creation of smart systems.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a simple and clear introduction to Artificial Intelligence for beginners. The site will explain what AI is, how it works, and how people can use AI in daily life, education, and business. The website will also include examples, beginner guides, and recommended tools to help users start working with AI and automation.

Scenarios

  • I am new to artificial intelligence. Where should I start?
  • How can I use AI tools to improve my productivity or studies?
  • What is the difference between AI, machine learning, and automation?
